🔬 Sensor Roles by Feature
🏃 Activity Monitoring
MPU-6050 — detects steps, lying/standing, grazing vs resting posture via accelerometer + gyroscope
Algorithm — activity classified in firmware using threshold logic every 10 sec
❤️ Health Monitoring
DS18B20 — body temperature probe inserted in ear canal or rectum
MAX30102 — optical PPG for heart rate and SpO₂ via skin contact
Piezo Mic — jaw vibration sensor for rumination count per hour
🌸 Reproduction & Breeding
LMT70 Probe — 0.1°C precision temperature sensor for vaginal temp drop (pre-calving) or rise (estrus)
MPU-6050 — restlessness behavior indicates heat or imminent calving
🌾 Feeding & Nutrition
MEMS Mic — rumination drop = reduced appetite, possible illness
IMU Posture — grazing time (head-down angle) vs idle time tracking
Cloud AI — daily grazing pattern trends flagged to farmer dashboard
📍 Location & Tracking
NEO-6M GPS — real-time coordinates sent every 5 min or on trigger
Geo-fence — boundary stored in firmware, alert if breached
⚡ Power Design
Solar + LiPo — daytime solar charging, night on battery
Deep Sleep — ESP32 sleeps between readings to conserve power
Target life — 7–10 days without sun, unlimited with solar exposure
