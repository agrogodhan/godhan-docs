PCB LAYERS: 4-layer board recommended · Layer 1=Signal · Layer 2=GND pour · Layer 3=3V3 pour · Layer 4=Signal
DECOUPLING: 100nF ceramic cap on every IC VCC pin · 10µF bulk cap near LM1117 output
I²C PULLUPS: Single 4.7kΩ on SDA + SCL sufficient for 2 devices at 400kHz fast mode
ADC NOISE: Place 100nF cap between LMT70 VOUT and GND close to GPIO9 pin · Ground pour under analog section
ANTENNA KEEPOUT: No copper pour, vias or traces under ESP32-S3 antenna area (marked on module datasheet)
WATERPROOFING: Conformal coat entire PCB · Use sealed probe connectors (JST-GH) for DS18B20 and LMT70 external probes
PCB SIZE: Target 60mm × 40mm to fit inside collar housing · All connectors on one edge for easy assembly
BATTERY PROTECTION: TP4056 module includes over-charge, over-discharge and short circuit protection built-in
