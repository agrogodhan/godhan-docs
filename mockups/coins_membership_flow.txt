# Jira Document – Godhan App

---

## Epic: Insemination & Calving Lifecycle
**As a farmer, I want to record inseminations, track pregnancy, and get reminders for calving, so that I can manage cattle health and milk yield.**

---

### Story 1: Record Insemination
- **As a** farmer  
- **When** I select a cattle (14–16 months+) and tap “Add Insemination”  
- **Then** I should be able to enter insemination date, bull details, and notes  

**Acceptance Criteria:**  
- [ ] Form captures insemination date, bull details.  
- [ ] Timeline entry created in cattle profile.  
- [ ] Pregnancy confirmation reminder scheduled at +75 days.  

---

### Story 2: Confirm Pregnancy
- **As a** farmer  
- **When** I get a reminder (70–90 days)  
- **Then** I can mark cattle as Pregnant or Not Pregnant  

**Acceptance Criteria:**  
- [ ] If marked Pregnant → Expected Calving Date auto-calculated.  
- [ ] If Not Pregnant → status updated, retry option shown.  
- [ ] Notification scheduled: 6-month care + 1-week calving reminder.  

---

### Story 3: Auto Calculate Expected Calving Date
- **As a** system  
- **When** pregnancy is confirmed  
- **Then** calculate expected calving date using gestation (283 days cow, 310 buffalo, etc).  

**Acceptance Criteria:**  
- [ ] Expected date displayed on insemination tab.  
- [ ] Badge shows “Calving due in X days”.  
- [ ] Farmer can see exact due date.  

---

### Story 4: 6-Month Pregnancy Care Plan
- **As a** farmer  
- **When** cattle reaches 6 months pregnancy  
- **Then** I get a notification with care plan  

**Acceptance Criteria:**  
- [ ] Notification delivered.  
- [ ] Care plan checklist visible (nutrition, housing, vet, supplies).  
- [ ] Farmer can tick off items and save progress.  
- [ ] If cattle is still milking, show reminder: “Stop milking 90 days before calving.”  

---

### Story 5: 1-Week Pre-Calving Reminder
- **As a** farmer  
- **When** calving is 1 week away  
- **Then** I should get a preparation checklist  

**Acceptance Criteria:**  
- [ ] Notification delivered with exact due date.  
- [ ] Checklist includes vet readiness, supplies, pen cleaning.  
- [ ] Farmer can mark “Ready for calving”.  

---

### Story 6: Record Calving Event
- **As a** farmer  
- **When** calving happens  
- **Then** I can record delivery, calf details, and health outcome  

**Acceptance Criteria:**  
- [ ] Form captures calving date, calf tag, gender, weight, notes.  
- [ ] Newborn calf profile auto-created & linked.  
- [ ] Cattle status changes to “Lactation 1 Active”.  

---

### Story 7: Notifications & Dry Period Reminder
- **As a** system  
- **When** cattle enters 6–7 months pregnancy and still marked as milking  
- **Then** send reminder to dry it off  

**Acceptance Criteria:**  
- [ ] Notification shows “Stop milking for next 90 days to improve yield in next lactation”.  
- [ ] Farmer can mark “Stopped milking” as confirmation.
